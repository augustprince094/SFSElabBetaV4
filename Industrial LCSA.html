Industrial Life Cycle Sustainability Assessment

Under this research theme, we conduct life cycle sustainability assessment taking into account environmental; social, economic and techno-efficiency assessment on product systems.


Our research activities under this theme include

  • Evaluate the magnitude and significance of the process-product life cycle and its associated environmental performance
  • Identify hotspots for improvement through life cycle assessment
  • Develop sustainability improvements scenarios and their respective environmental and economic implications

On-going Projects

Techno-eco-environmental Assessments

(a) Investigating the techno-eco environmental assessment of pea protein fractionation

(b) Measuring the industrial eco-efficiency assessment of Hybrid Fractionation for pea protein and processing of pork.

(c) Exploring the life cycle assessment of the production of biopesticides with key focus on (i) contribution on environmental impact categories (ii) Processing benchmark analysis (iii) Hotspot analysis.



Sustainability Assessment of feed additive production

(a) Investigating the environmental impact of commercial feed addtive production systems.

(b) Measuring the social life cycle ssessment of feed additive production systems on key stakeholders within the value chain (with key focus on farm level use and production stage).

(c) Investigating the technoeconomic and environmental ife cycle ssessment of the commercial production of promising methane emission reduction feed additives.
