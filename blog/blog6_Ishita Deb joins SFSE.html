Summar and Fall 2023


Graduate Student, Mr. Vincent Abe-Inge presents the CIFST conference between 7-9th June 2023 at London, Ontario

July 23, 2019

On May 2, 2023, the SFSE Research group welcomed a new graduate student to team-Miss Ishita Deb.

Miss Deb holds a BSc in Agricultural Engineering and has extensive knowledge and experience at food processing and smart farming technology.

Miss Deb worked on Circular Economy research space with support from Mr. Raphael Aidoo on Processing Modelling and Sustainability Assessment of Biopesticide Production from Pea Starch.

Miss Deb is passionate about Agriculture and Sustainability, Waste valorization, and Environmental Conservation. In her leisure time, she prefers painting and playing cricket.
